About me

We, at UTOPIA tours and advise , provide our customers with tourism and travel related services in Kerala. We have over two decades of experience in providing services for tourists coming from different parts of the world and hence, we have knowledge of the best sites to visit in the region along with the travellers requirements and taste.

Kerala is one of the most beautiful states in India and it is called 'God's own country' because of the abundance of natural beauty in this land. We create flexible tour plans according to our customer's requirement and strive to provide the best travel experience for them to enjoy a memorable holiday in Kerala.

Our trips are completely based around your interests and schedule. Our expertise, knowledge, and personal relationships allow us to design journeys that will let you have a perfect holiday experience. We capture the serenity of the backwaters of Kerala ,also spell bounding natural beauty of hill stations like Munnar, and many other breathtaking sites. UTOPIA tours and advise ensures that your journey is seamless, authentic, and beyond what you had imagined.

- CEO  Navas
